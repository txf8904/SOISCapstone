Meet your friend in Tenais

New Detective

Main character of the game, the player.

She born in Tenais, when she was eight years old a family accident happened that made her left Tenais.

"What is right, and what is wrong? Who is the angel, and who is the devil?"

Nick

Detective's Partner

Nick is the first person that the player meets in this town. As a partner, Nick may not help inferring the truth, but he is willing to get everything you need. Also, Nick knows every road in town.

“I just want to live my day without worry.”

Captain Holt

Captain of Police department

Holt has been police captain of Tenais for decay. Holt has her unique view on the case, but she doesn't talk about it too much. That's might be the reason Holt can sit in that chair for a long time.
“There is no absolute evil nor absolute justice in this world ”

M.E. Ted

Medical Examiner

Ted is a workaholic, and he looks like a cold and inhumane. In fact, he cares a lot. Ted goes to the orphanage for free consultation in his free time, he gives his respect to every victim on his table, and he cares about the justice.
“The body is the victim's last word.”

George

Crime-scene investigator

George is enthusiastic and active. He is a little bit hot head, especially when the victim is a minor.
“The bad guys deserve the punishment they deserve, even if it is above the law”
